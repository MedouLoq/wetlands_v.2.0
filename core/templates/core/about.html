About GeoWetlands Mauritania

Protecting and preserving Mauritania's valuable wetland ecosystems

Our Mission

GeoWetlands Mauritania is dedicated to the conservation, sustainable management, and scientific understanding of Mauritania's wetland ecosystems.

Through comprehensive data collection, monitoring, and stakeholder engagement, we aim to protect these vital habitats for future generations while supporting sustainable development and climate resilience.

Our platform serves as a centralized repository of information on wetland sites, biodiversity, threats, and conservation measures, enabling informed decision-making and collaborative action.

Key Features

Discover the tools and resources available on our platform

Comprehensive Database

Access detailed information on wetland sites, species inventories, threats, and conservation measures across Mauritania.

Interactive Mapping

Visualize wetland sites geographically with our interactive mapping system, enabling spatial analysis and planning.

Monitoring & Reporting

Track changes in wetland conditions, biodiversity, and conservation status over time with our monitoring tools.
